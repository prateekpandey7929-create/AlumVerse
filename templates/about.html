About AlumVerse

AI-powered alumni networking and career development tracking system for Indore Institute of Science & Technology.

Connecting IIST Generation Lines

AlumVerse bridges the gap between current students and graduating batches. Using clean database feeds and AI-based matcher models, our application links students with specific alumni professionals based on skills, career track alignments and goals.

Whether you want to calculate your resume's ATS compatibility score, run mock career path validations, trace technical skill gaps, or message verified company leads, AlumVerse gives IIST members a centralized portal for growth.
