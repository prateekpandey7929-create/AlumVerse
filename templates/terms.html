Terms of Service

Guidelines and rules for using the Indore Institute of Science & Technology alumni engagement portal.

User Verification & Access

By accessing AlumVerse, you verify that the graduation details, branch selection, and professional records you submit are accurate. Students and alumni are expected to keep their contact cards up to date.

Professional Engagement Standards

All conversations, mentorship booking sessions, and questions sent through the chat system must maintain professional and academic etiquette. Harassment, spam, or false internship postings are strictly prohibited.

Account Restrictions

IIST administration panels reserve the right to audit connection attempts, verify registration details, and restrict access for accounts found violating these guidelines.
