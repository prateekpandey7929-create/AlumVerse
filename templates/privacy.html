Privacy Policy

Your privacy and database security are critical foundations of the AlumVerse portal.

Information Collection & Storage

AlumVerse respects your privacy. All personal details including academic years, verification credentials, profile photos, contact details, resume uploads and communication chats are securely stored and encrypted inside the platform's PostgreSQL database.

Usage Scope

The data collected is utilized strictly for professional networking, career mentorship recommendations, skill gap analysis, job opportunity matching, and educational engagement within Indore Institute of Science & Technology's verified members network.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share personal profile details with third-party external networks without explicit user approval. All security settings can be audited from your profile configuration page.
