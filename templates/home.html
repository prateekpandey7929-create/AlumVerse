About AlumVerse

AlumVerse is an AI-enabled alumni tracking and engagement platform designed for Indore Institute of Science and Technology. The system helps students connect with alumni, discover mentorship opportunities, analyze their resumes, and identify skill gaps required for career growth.

Alumni Directory

AI Alumni Recommendation

Resume Analyzer

Upload your resume and receive an ATS score with improvement suggestions.

Mentorship Booking

Schedule mentorship sessions with experienced alumni for career guidance.
